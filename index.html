ABOUT ME

I spent the last 20 years leading sales teams of 300+ people, closing more than $200MM in net new ARR for technology firms. Then, in 2019, completely burned out, I quit my lucrative VP Sales job with a hyper-growth SaaS company and did absolutely nothing for 5 months. Rested and refreshed, I have since launched a fast-growing label printing business, and now have time freedom to do what I love: Teaching people how to use the same sales strategies I used to grow my career, to grow their own.
